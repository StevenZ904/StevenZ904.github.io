I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [Arizona State University](https://www.asu.edu/), co-advised by [Prof. Chitta Baral](https://www.public.asu.edu/~cbaral/) and [Prof. Ruoyu Wang](https://ruoyuwang.me/). I expect to complete my PhD in Fall 2028.

Previously, I received my M.S. in AI Engineering (Information Security) from [Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.cmu.edu/) in 2024, and my B.A. in Computer Science and Economics from [Boston College](https://www.bc.edu/) in 2022.

## Research Interests

My general research interests lie in **Natural Language Processing** and its applications. My recent work focuses on:

- **LLM Evaluation** -- benchmarking and understanding large language models
- **Code Generation** -- LLM-assisted software engineering tasks
- **AI for Software Security** -- applying AI methods to binary security and vulnerability analysis
- **Agents** -- multi-agent systems and agentic workflows
